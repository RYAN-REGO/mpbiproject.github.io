EVENT : IDEA TO PROTOTYPE

Rules an d Regulations

Participation  : Participate individually or in group of maximum 4 members.

❖ EVENT RULES

    1) Idea should be submitted in the form of concept note, not more than 2 pages.
    2) Team size: Individual or team of maximum 4 students. 
    3) Idea should be written by considering the following points : 
         a) Objective of the idea 
         b) Technical Feasibility 
         c) Operational Feasibility 
         d) Financial Feasibility 
         e) Societal Benefit

    4) IDEA MAY BE FOR :
    a) Engineering Field (Preferably interdisciplinary area) addressing to the                                solution of daily life problems or any social requirement. 
    b) It may be a thought of developing a new product or method of analysis                              or design. 
    c) Improvement in existing product or method of analysis or design.
    d) Product development by using new materials or waste materials, or                                      resources including energy management to improve sustainability,                                      safety, and security etc. 
     e) Any new GAME development 
     f) Any other aspect

5) Concept Note Format (A4 Pages, not more than 2 pages)  
  a) Healing Project Prototype
  b) Name of Innovators, Name 0f Mentor (If Any), Class and Roll  Nos
  c) Title of the Idea 
  d) Introduction 
  e) Brief Description with a Sketch    (if Possible) 
  f) Goal & Objective 
  g) Time Required to Develop the    Prototype 
  h) Approximate Cost Structure 
  i) Unique Value Proposition 
  j) Business Proposed
